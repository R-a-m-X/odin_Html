Shredded Potato Salad

Description

Looking for a simple salad? Try this Shredded Potato Salad that's prepared with a simple dressing made with sweet chilli sauce, caster sugar, pepper sauce, and extra virgin olive oil. It's a delicious salad recipe that can be enjoyed in Lunch as well as Dinner. Do try this amazing potato salad. (Recipe by: Ritu Oswal)

Ingredients

  • tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • juice of 1 lemon as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon caster sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sweet chilli sauce
  • salt as required
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper sauce

Recipe preparation steps

  1. Shred the potatoes

    2. To prepare this recipe, peel and grate the potatoes. Wash them in normal water 2-3 times.

  2. Soak in hot water

    3. Put the grated potatoes in hot/boiling water and take them out in approximately 5 min.

  3. Prepare dressing

    4. Now, prepare the dressing for the salad by mixing together all the dressing ingredients in a small bowl.

  4. Toss the salad

    5. Take out the grated potatoes in a bowl and pour this dressing over it. Toss and enjoy this simple salad in lunch.
